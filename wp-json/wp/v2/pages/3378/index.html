\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tThe Cartagena O-Key program aims to promote employment among the young population in Cartagena, by improving their knowledge of the english language. If you have english skills, sign up for the call that will strengthen your knowledge and connect you with job opportunities in the BPO sector in Cartagena, to improve the quality of life of young people and boost the economy in the city. \t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
