The Promigas Foundation is present in each of the operating territories of Promigas and its companies: we are present in 12 departments of Colombia that represent 40% of the national territory and different regions in Peru. As such, we continue to contribute to the sustainable development of the territories, adding energy, talents, and resources to reach more regions and support the well-being of the communities located in our areas of influence.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
